At Tejas Equipment Rentals, we provide top-notch construction rental equipment and tools for contractors, builders, and businesses across Texas. From earthmoving to aerial and everything in between, we make sure our customers have the right tools to get the job done safely, efficiently, and on time. With convenient locations and a responsive delivery network, we serve the Lone Star State with reliability and pride.

Our Locations

Our Story

Founded in 1975, Tejas has been family-owned and operated for over five decades. What started as a local operation in South Texas has grown into the trusted name in equipment rentals across Texas. Through every generation, one thing has stayed the same: our commitment to honest work, dependable service, and treating our customers like family.


What Makes Us Different

Our team represents some of the most knowledgeable and seasoned professionals in the industry — many with decades of hands-on experience in construction and equipment operations. We don't just rent machines; we understand the work you're doing and what it takes to get it done right. That expertise, combined with our family values and commitment to service, is what keeps customers coming back to Tejas.


But what truly sets us apart is how we see and treat our customers — like partners, not transactions. When you rent from Tejas, you become part of our extended family. We take pride in learning your business, anticipating your needs, and standing behind you from the first call to the final pickup. Our roots run deep in the communities we serve, and we stay actively involved — supporting local schools, events, and causes that keep Texas strong. For us, success isn't just measured in jobs completed, but in relationships built and trust earned.
